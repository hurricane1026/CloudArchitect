## Books

### Cloud Architecture

- **"Designing Data-Intensive Applications"** by Martin Kleppmann
  - Deep dive into data systems, distributed systems, and scalability

- **"Cloud Native Patterns"** by Cornelia Davis
  - Patterns for building resilient cloud applications

- **"Building Microservices"** by Sam Newman
  - Comprehensive guide to microservices architecture

- **"Site Reliability Engineering"** by Google
  - Google's approach to running large-scale systems

- **"The Phoenix Project"** by Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, and George Spafford
  - DevOps novel illustrating IT transformation
